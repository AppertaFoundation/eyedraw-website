Description:

This element handles the editing and display of the history of the presenting complaint. For new patients this would include a detailed account of the patient's symptoms and any other directly related information. NB Previous medical history, medications etc are entered elsewhere in the system.

Text can be typed directly into the text box, which expands vertically to accommodate additional lines. Alternatively items of the history (chosen to be relevant to the specialty) can be selected from the drop down menus.
