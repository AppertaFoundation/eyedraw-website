Description:

This element collects the social history of the patient, and covers a subset of items from 6.01 to 6.15 of the National Glaucoma Dataset

Please note that items 6.01 to 6.03 (occupation of parents) which are currently mandatory items are not included. Items 6.05, 6.08, 6.09, 6.11, and 6.12 which are currently non-mandatory ARE included.

Type of job is a general text box to cover text for the option 'Other' in the Occupation drop down, and also job type
